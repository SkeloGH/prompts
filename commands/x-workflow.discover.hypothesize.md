## Purpose

Problem Discovery — Hypothesis Generation step. Write ideas as sentences that explain what is going on.

## Execution

- Generate ideas from your analysis
- Write each as a sentence explaining what is going on
- Hypothesis may be wrong or right—not important at this point
- Use Taguchi diagrams to organize and prioritize causes
- Externalize and organize ideas to find the right ones

## Guidelines

- Keep hypotheses as single sentences
- Establish Experimental Design and Tests before moving to Problem Definition
