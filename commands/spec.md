General guidelines for specs: [../../docs/guides/SPECCING.md](../../docs/guides/SPECCING.md).

Clearing out details has a high priority. Dissambiguate and ask clarifying questions as much as needed.

While iterating reviews, present only the excerpts that are relevant to the current review instead of presenting the entire spec.

Not all specs need to have all sections. Only include sections for which details have been provided and are relevant or are mandatory for the spec to be valid.
