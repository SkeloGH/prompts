## Purpose

Problem Discovery — Data Analysis step. Use data to prove ideas wrong or right.

## Execution

- Use the data you have to test your ideas
- Go through the data even if you think you're 100% confident—you may find surprises
- Use visualizations, models, text/log analysis tools
- Anything is valid as long as you understand the data and can test your ideas

## Guidelines

- Do not skip—surprises often emerge here
- Document findings for the hypothesize step
