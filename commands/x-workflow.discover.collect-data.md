## Purpose

Problem Discovery — Data Gathering step. Gather data historically to understand when and how the problem manifests.

## Execution

- When did the problem manifest?
- What was going on at the time?
- What variables on the system got modified? (one or more?)
- Think logical and simple explanations
- Match explanations against the data you have

## Guidelines

- Look at data historically
- Document findings for the analyze step
