## Purpose

Problem Discovery — Observation step. Never take what you know for granted. Observe the system and understand its structure or flow.

## Execution

- List logs, reports, and data from peers
- Identify system structure and flow
- Note what draws attention: new project, customer use case, bug, undiscovered flow, performance hit

## Guidelines

- Do not assume prior knowledge
- Document what you observe for the next step (collect-data)
